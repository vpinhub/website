Future Pinball Tables

Featuring Terry Red

The amazing Terry Red continues to breathe new life into Future Pinball! Always giving proper credit to the original table authors, Terry adds his magic to each table he touches. From adding BAM Edition, to PinEvent V2, and now the FizX Mod. The results are excellent tables that play great with modern dynamic lighting, shadowmaps, ray cast shadows, and full cabinet support.

On top of that, Terry never leaves you on your own to figure out how to get his FP tables up and running, his step by step video and written guides are some of the best out there! Learn how to play some of our favorite Terry's tables below.
